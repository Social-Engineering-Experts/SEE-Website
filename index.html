About

SEETF is a cybersecurity Capture the Flag competition hosted by the Social Engineering Experts CTF team.

Categories include web exploitation, binary exploitation, reverse engineering, forensics, cryptography and more.

Registration starts in May 2022. Join our Discord server for updates.
