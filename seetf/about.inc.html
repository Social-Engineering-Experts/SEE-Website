About

SEETF is a cybersecurity Capture the Flag competition hosted by the Social Engineering Experts CTF team.

Categories include web exploitation, binary exploitation, reverse engineering, forensics, cryptography and more.

Registration starts in May 2022. Join our Discord server for updates.

Disclaimer: This is not a CTF about social engineering! We do not condone the use of social engineering techniques during the competition.

Cyber League

SEETF is a Cyber League community CTF partner.

By participating in SEETF, you will:

  • Stand to win attractive SEETF prizes
  • Earn points for Cyber League to advance your team to the finals*

*Please note that your team must register for the Cyber League.

Participation in Cyber League is only open to Singaporean teams, and is completely optional. Anyone can participate in SEETF without participating in Cyber League.
